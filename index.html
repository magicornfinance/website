How to Buy

Follow Simple Steps For Buying $MANU

Download & setup MetaMask or TrustWallet

Download MetaMask (a crypto wallet in the form of a browser extension) or TrustWallet (an app for your phone). After that you will have to add the Binance Smart Chain to your network-list.

Set Up Your Account

Buy BNB on an exchange (i.e. Binance, Coinbase etc.). Transfer the tokens to your MetaMask wallet address. BEP-20 addresses start with a “0x”.

Go to PancakeSwap and Swap for $MANU

Click here to head on over to Pancakeswap or use this address 0x4Af123D5a3fB1FE0Df504EF7F2246f0B8eef7A4D to select Magicorn Inu. Set the slippage tolerance to 20%.
